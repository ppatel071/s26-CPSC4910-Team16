Sponsor Bulk Upload

Upload a pipe-delimited text file to create sponsor users or add drivers to your organization.




Format

<type>|organization name|first name|last name|email address|points|reason for points

  • Use D for drivers and S for sponsor users.
  • Leave the organization field blank. Sponsor uploads always target your organization.
  • If points are supplied for a driver, a reason is required.
  • Points on sponsor records are ignored and reported as warnings.
  • New users are created with username equal to email. Temporary passwords for newly created users appear below after the upload completes.

Example

D||Joe|Driver|me@email.com||

S||Jill|Sponsor|jill@mail.com|100|New Driver

D||Tom|Smith|tom887@gmail.com

D||New|Driver|some@email.com
